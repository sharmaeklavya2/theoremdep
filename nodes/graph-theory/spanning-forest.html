Spanning forest

Dependencies:

  • Forest
  • Spanning tree

The spanning forest of an undirected graph G is the union of the spanning trees of the connected components of G. This means that the spanning forest of a graph exists iff the spanning tree of every connected component exists.

Dependency for:

  • DFS on undirected graph gives spanning forest
