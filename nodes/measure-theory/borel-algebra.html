Borel algebra

Dependencies:

  1. Generated σ-algebra
  2. /analysis/topological-space

Let $T$ be a topological space. Let $\tau$ be the set of all open sets of $T$. The Borel algebra on $T$, denoted as $\mathcal{B}(T)$, is defined as $\mathcal{B}(T) = \sigma(\tau)$.

A common example is $\mathcal{B}(\mathbb{R})$, since $\mathbb{R}$ is a topological space.
