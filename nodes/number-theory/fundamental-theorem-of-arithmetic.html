Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic

Dependencies:

  • Euclid's lemma /number-theory/euclids-lemma Used in proof
  • Every number has a prime factorization /number-theory/every-number-has-prime-factorization Used in proof

Proof

$n = p_1p_2\ldots = q_1q_2\ldots$

By Euclid's lemma, every $p_i$ must divide a $q_j$. We can prove this theorem using mathematical induction.
