Geometric bin packing: DFF transform (incomplete)

Dependencies: (incomplete)

Let $I$ be a $d$-dimensional geometric bin packing instance. Let $f_1, f_2, ..., f_d$ be DFFs. Let $J$ be the bin packing instance where for each item in $I$, its $i^{\textrm{th}}$ dimension $x$ is replaced by $f_i(x)$. $J$ is called the DFF transform of $I$.

If $I$ can fit in 1 bin, then so can $J$.

This is mentioned as lemma 2.3 in [caprara2008packing], which includes references to proofs.
