Dependencies:

  1. Bin packing and Knapsack

There is an FPTAS for the knapsack problem. See Algorithm 3.2 in the book 'The Design of Approximation Algorithms' by Williamson and Shmoys.

The running time is $O(n^3/\epsilon)$.
