Polynomial divisibility

Dependencies:

  • Polynomial

A polynomial $p(x)$ is divisible in $F[x]$ by $q(x)$ iff there exists a polynomial $r(x)$ such that $p(x), q(x), r(x) \in F[x]$ and $p(x) = q(x)r(x)$.

  • q(x)F[x] is in p(x)F[x] iff p(x) divides q(x)
