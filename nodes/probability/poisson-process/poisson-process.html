Poisson process

Dependencies:

  1. Counting process
  2. Poisson distribution

A poisson process with parameter $\lambda$ is a counting process $\{N(t): t \ge 0\}$ with independent and stationary increments such that $N(t) \sim \operatorname{Poisson}(\lambda t)$.
