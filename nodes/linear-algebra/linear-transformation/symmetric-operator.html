Symmetric operator

Dependencies:

  • Linear transformation
  • Inner product space

Let $L: V \mapsto V$ be a linear transformation over an inner product space.

$L$ is a symmetric operator iff $(\forall u, v \in V, \langle L(u), v \rangle = \langle u, L(v) \rangle)$.

Dependency for:

  • Symmetric operator iff hermitian
  • Symmetric operator on V has a basis of orthonormal eigenvectors
  • All eigenvalues of a symmetric operator are real
