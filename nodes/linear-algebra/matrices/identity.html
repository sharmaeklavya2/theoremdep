Identity matrix

Dependencies:

  1. Semiring
  2. Matrix

Let $R$ be a semiring with unity. The identity matrix of size $n$, denoted as $I_n$, is a matrix in $\mathbb{M}_{n, n}(R)$ such that \[ I_n[i, j] = \begin{cases} 1 & i = j \\ 0 & i \neq j \end{cases} \]
