Positive semidefiniteness

Dependencies:

  1. Matrix

Let $A$ be an $n$ by $n$ matrix over $\mathbb{R}$. Then $A$ is positive semidefinite iff \[ \forall u \in \mathbb{R}^n, u^TAu \ge 0 \]
