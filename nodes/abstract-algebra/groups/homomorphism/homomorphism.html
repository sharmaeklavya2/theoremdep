Homomorphism on groups

Dependencies:

  • Group

Let $G$ be a group. Let $\phi: G \mapsto G$ be a function. Then $\phi$ is a homomorphism iff $\forall a, b \in G, \phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)$.
