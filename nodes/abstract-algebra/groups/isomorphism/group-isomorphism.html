Isomorphism on Groups

Dependencies:

  1. Group

Groups $G_1$ and $G_2$ are isomorphic ($G \cong H$) iff there is a bijection $\phi$ from $G_1$ to $G_2$ such that $\phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)$. $\phi$ is called an isomorphism.
