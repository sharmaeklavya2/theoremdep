Identity of a group is unique.

Proof

Let $e_1$ and $e_2$ be identities of $G$.

  • Since $e_1$ is identity, $e_1 * e_2 = e_2$.
  • Since $e_2$ is identity, $e_1 * e_2 = e_1$.

Therefore, $e_1 = e_2$.
