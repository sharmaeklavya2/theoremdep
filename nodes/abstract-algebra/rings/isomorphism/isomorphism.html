Ring isomorphism

Dependencies:

  1. Ring

A ring isomorphism is a bijective function $\phi: R_1 \mapsto R_2$ where $R_1$ and $R_2$ are rings, $\phi(a+b) = \phi(a) + \phi(b)$ and $\phi(ab) = \phi(a)\phi(b)$.
